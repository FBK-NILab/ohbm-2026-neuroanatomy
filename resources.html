📚Materials and Instructions

The hands-on session actively engages participants in exploring human brain anatomy through advanced neuroimaging data. In addition to visualizing the organization of human brain connectivity, participants will interactively identify and recognize principal anatomical structures using multimodal datasets and dedicated visualization tools.

Before the event

Prerequisites

To participate in the hands-on session, attendees are required to:

  • Bring their own laptop computer.
  • Download and install all required materials before the event.
  • Verify in advance that the software is correctly installed and functioning on their system.

Preparing the materials ahead of time will ensure a smooth and productive learning experience during the session.
