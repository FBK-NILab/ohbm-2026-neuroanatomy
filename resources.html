📚Materials and Instructions

Morning lectures will be followed in the afternoon by dedicated hands-on sessions. The hands-on component is specifically designed to provide practical training in the virtual dissection of human brain connectivity using tractography. Participants will have the opportunity to explore and manipulate white-matter pathways in an interactive environment, reinforcing their understanding through guided practice.

To support these activities, the organization will provide access to a dedicated tool for tractography manipulation, along with a curated repertoire of annotated datasets. These resources will enable participants to work with high-quality data and follow structured exercises throughout the session.

Further practical details, including technical requirements and step-by-step instructions for participation, will be made available on this webpage in the weeks leading up to the event.
